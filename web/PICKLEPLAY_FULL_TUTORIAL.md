# PicklePlay Full Tutorial

This document is a detailed user guide for using PicklePlay.
It is written as a practical walkthrough for real users of the web app, from first login up to role-specific workflows like court owner, court manager, coach, and admin tasks.

## What PicklePlay Is

PicklePlay is a multi-purpose pickleball platform that can be used to:

- create an account and manage your player profile
- discover courts and book playing schedules
- track your bookings and view receipts
- join the community feed and groups
- find playing partners and send invites
- join squads or teams
- browse and join tournaments
- explore coaches, clinics, guides, and academy content
- manage role-specific work if you are a coach, court owner, court manager, or admin

## Important Notes Before You Start

1. The menus you see depend on your account role and enabled features.
2. Some users will see only player features, while others will also see owner, manager, coach, or admin tools.
3. Guests can browse some parts of the app, but a login is required before a real booking can be completed.
4. Some sign-in methods may require 2-factor verification before you can continue.
5. Some pages may be hidden if the feature is temporarily disabled by maintenance settings.
6. In some parts of the app, the URL uses `teams` while the UI label says `Squads`. These refer to the same feature area.

## 1. Creating Your Account

### Option A: Sign up with email

1. Open PicklePlay.
2. Go to `Sign Up`.
3. Enter your name, email address, and password.
4. Agree to the Terms and any required consent.
5. Submit the form.
6. Check your email inbox for the verification message.
7. Open the verification email and complete the email confirmation step.
8. Return to PicklePlay and log in.

### Option B: Sign up with Google or Facebook

1. Open `Sign Up`.
2. Choose `Google` or `Facebook`.
3. Continue with the provider login flow.
4. Approve access when prompted.
5. Finish the callback process and return to PicklePlay.

### If the app asks you to set a username

After social sign-in, PicklePlay may ask you to complete a username setup step.

1. Enter the username you want to use.
2. Review your name if needed.
3. Save the profile.

This usually happens when the social account is new and your profile still has no username.

## 2. Logging In

### Standard login

1. Open `Login`.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Submit the form.
4. If the app recognizes a pending redirect, it may automatically send you back to the page you originally wanted to open.

### Login with Google or Facebook

1. Open `Login`.
2. Choose your social sign-in provider.
3. Complete the provider flow.
4. Return to PicklePlay and continue.

### Email verification reminder

If you signed up with email but did not verify your email yet, PicklePlay may block the session and ask you to verify first.

## 3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Some login flows require a second verification step before the rest of the app becomes available.

### How it works

1. Log in normally.
2. If 2FA is required, the app sends you to the verification screen.
3. Enter the verification code sent through the configured 2FA flow.
4. If available, you may also use a backup code.
5. Submit the code to continue into the app.

### Important 2FA notes

- If 2FA is pending, many pages will redirect you back to the verification screen.
- Different sign-in providers can behave differently. For example, some Google-based flows may not follow the same 2FA path as email/password.

## 4. Resetting or Changing Your Password

### Forgot password

1. Open `Login`.
2. Click `Forgot Password`.
3. Enter your email address.
4. Submit the request.
5. Open the reset link from your email.
6. Complete the password reset flow.
7. Return to the app and log in again.

### Change password while logged in

1. Open `Profile`.
2. Go to your security settings if available.
3. Start the password update flow.
4. Complete any required re-authentication step.
5. Save the new password.

## 7. Booking a Court

Booking is one of the main PicklePlay flows.

### Step 1: Open the booking area

1. Go to `Booking`.
2. Browse the available locations and courts.
3. Use search, filters, and location-based discovery as needed.

Common filters may include:

- court type
- price range
- amenities
- nearby or location-based choices

### Step 2: Choose a location or court

1. Select the location you want.
2. Review court details, availability, and schedule.
3. If needed, open the dedicated court page from `Court Detail`.

### Step 3: Pick your date and time

1. Choose the playing date.
2. Select an available time slot.
3. Confirm the selected court and schedule.

### Step 4: Choose a payment method

Depending on the court owner settings, available methods may include:

- Cash
- GCash
- Maya

### Step 5: If using GCash or Maya

1. Enter the payment reference number.
2. Upload proof of payment if the flow asks for it.
3. Review the details before submission.

### Step 6: Confirm the booking

1. Review the court, date, time, and payment details.
2. Submit the booking.
3. Wait for the booking status to update based on the venue's workflow.

### Important booking notes

- Guests can browse the booking UI, but the app requires login before the booking is actually created.
- If you are a court owner and want to book like a normal player, you may need to switch back to `Player` mode first.
- Some payment methods stay in a pending state until the payment proof is reviewed.

## 8. Viewing Court Details

The `Court Detail` page is useful when you want a more focused view of one court.

You can use it to:

- review the selected court

- review booking-related information in a more direct page flow

If you tap a booking action there without being logged in, PicklePlay may redirect you to `Login`.

## 9. Managing Your Bookings

Open `My Bookings` to review bookings after submission.

### What you can usually do in My Bookings

- see booking status
- review payment state
- open booking receipts
- track invitation-related items
- resubmit payment proof if requested

### Understanding booking status

A booking can move through different states depending on venue review, payment verification, or schedule timing.

Examples include:

- pending
- confirmed
- payment under review
- other venue-specific states

### If your payment proof is rejected or needs changes

1. Open `My Bookings`.
2. Find the affected booking.
3. Look for the proof resubmission action if available.
4. Upload the corrected payment proof.
5. Save and wait for re-review.

### If you need to cancel or reschedule

Available actions depend on the current UI, booking status, and venue rules.
If the button is not visible in your booking screen, contact the court directly or use the support process defined by the venue.

## 24. Common Issues and What To Do

### I cannot access a page

Possible reasons:

- you are not logged in
- 2FA is still pending
- your role does not have access
- the feature is disabled for your role
- the app is in maintenance mode

### I signed up but I still cannot log in fully

Check the following:

1. Verify your email.
2. Retry login.
3. Complete 2FA if requested.

### I can browse booking, but I cannot finalize the booking

This usually means you still need to log in before the booking record can be created.

### I do not see owner, manager, or coach menus

These menus appear only when:

- your account has the correct approved role
- the feature is enabled for that role
- you have switched into the correct active role if role switching is supported

### I do not see a booking receipt or confirmed payment yet

Some bookings remain pending until payment verification is completed by the venue workflow.

### I used a social login and something feels incomplete

Your account may still need:

- username setup
- email completion
- 2FA completion depending on the sign-in path
