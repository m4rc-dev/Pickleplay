# PicklePlay Player Guide

This guide is for regular PicklePlay users who want to play, book courts, join the community, find partners, and join tournaments.

## Who This Guide Is For

Use this guide if your main PicklePlay activities are:

- creating an account
- logging in and securing your profile
- booking courts
- tracking bookings and receipts
- using community features
- finding partners
- joining groups, squads, and tournaments
- browsing coaches, guides, and academy content

## Before You Start

1. Some features only appear when they are enabled for your account.
2. Guests can browse some pages, but login is required before a real booking is created.
3. If 2-factor authentication is required, many pages will redirect you to the verification screen until it is completed.
4. In some parts of the app, the route says `teams` while the UI says `Squads`.

## 1. Create Your Account

### Sign up with email

1. Open PicklePlay.
2. Click `Sign Up`.
3. Enter your name, email, and password.
4. Accept the required terms.
5. Submit the form.
6. Open the verification email.
7. Confirm your email.
8. Return to PicklePlay and log in.

### Sign up with Google or Facebook

1. Open `Sign Up`.
2. Choose `Google` or `Facebook`.
3. Complete the provider login flow.
4. Return to PicklePlay after the callback finishes.

### If you are asked to set a username

1. Enter your preferred username.
2. Review your profile name.
3. Save the changes.

This usually happens for new social accounts that do not have a completed PicklePlay profile yet.

## 2. Log In

### Standard login

1. Open `Login`.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Submit the form.
4. If the app remembers the page you wanted to visit, it may redirect you there automatically.

### Social login

1. Open `Login`.
2. Choose your sign-in provider.
3. Complete the provider flow.
4. Return to the app.

### If email verification is still pending

PicklePlay may stop the session and ask you to verify your email before continuing.

## 3. Complete 2FA If Prompted

Some accounts or sign-in flows require a second verification step.

1. Log in.
2. If redirected to the 2FA screen, enter the code you received.
3. If backup codes are available, use one if needed.
4. Submit the code.
5. Continue into the app.

## 4. Reset or Change Your Password

### Forgot password

1. Open `Login`.
2. Click `Forgot Password`.
3. Enter your email.
4. Submit the request.
5. Open the password reset link from your email.
6. Set your new password.
7. Log in again.

### Change password while logged in

1. Open `Profile`.
2. Go to your security settings.
3. Start the password change flow.
4. Complete the re-authentication step if required.
5. Save the new password.

## 6. Book a Court

### Step 1: Open the booking page

1. Go to `Booking`.
2. Browse locations and courts.
3. Use filters like price, amenities, court type, or nearby options.

### Step 2: Choose a court

1. Pick a location.
2. Review the court details and schedule.
3. If needed, open the dedicated `Court Detail` page.

### Step 3: Select date and time

1. Choose your playing date.
2. Select an available time slot.
3. Confirm the schedule you want.

### Step 4: Choose payment method

Available options may include:

- Cash
- GCash
- Maya

### Step 5: Complete payment details

If using GCash or Maya:

1. Enter the payment reference number.
2. Upload proof of payment if requested.
3. Review the details.

### Step 6: Submit the booking

1. Review the booking summary.
2. Confirm the booking.
3. Wait for the booking status to update.

### Important player booking notes

- You can browse booking pages as a guest, but login is required before the booking is actually created.
- Some payment methods stay pending until the venue verifies the payment proof.
- If you open a booking action from `Court Detail` while not logged in, the app may send you to `Login`.

## 7. Use My Bookings

Open `My Bookings` after submitting a reservation.

### What you can do there

- review booking status
- check payment status
- open receipts
- track invitation-related items
- resubmit payment proof if requested

### If a payment proof needs correction

1. Open `My Bookings`.
2. Find the affected booking.
3. Use the resubmission action if available.
4. Upload the corrected proof.
5. Save and wait for re-review.

## 8. View and Use Receipts

Receipts may be available from:

- `My Bookings`
- post-booking success flows
- booking-related dialogs

### A receipt may include

- booking ID
- court name
- date and time
- booking status
- QR code when applicable

### Available actions

- view
- print
- download as an image

## 9. Use the Community

The `Community` section is your social activity hub.

### Common actions

- read posts
- create posts
- comment on posts
- explore tags or community activity
- move into related partner and group experiences

### Basic workflow

1. Open `Community`.
2. Browse the feed.
3. Create a post if posting is available to you.
4. Comment or interact with content.

## 10. Join and Use Groups

### Find a group

1. Open `Groups`.
2. Search for a group.
3. Review the group details.
4. Join if it matches your interests.

### Create a group

If group creation is enabled for you:

1. Start the create group flow.
2. Enter the group name and description.
3. Save the group.
4. Add or invite members.

### Inside a group

A group page may let you:

- see members
- view events
- RSVP
- read or send group messages
- manage the group if you are an organizer

## 11. Find Playing Partners

Use `Partners` to discover players and connect with them.

### Typical actions

- search for players
- filter players by matching criteria
- send match requests
- review received requests
- track sent requests
- send play invites

### Basic workflow

1. Open `Partners`.
2. Use search or filters.
3. Review player cards.
4. Send a request or invite.
5. Track responses in the tabs.

## 12. Use Messages

### How to message

1. Open `Messages`.
2. Pick a conversation.
3. Read the thread.
4. Send a message if the thread is open for chat.

### Important note

Some conversations may begin as request-style interactions instead of full open chat.

## 13. Join Tournaments

### Browse tournaments

1. Open `Tournaments`.
2. Browse or filter the tournament list.
3. Open a tournament card for details.

### On the tournament page

You may be able to:

- review tournament details
- register
- withdraw
- view bracket-related information

Some tournaments are individual-based while others depend on squad participation.

## 14. Join or Manage Squads

### What squads are for

- team organization
- member invites
- roster management
- tournament participation when allowed

### Basic squad workflow

1. Open `Squads`.
2. Create a squad or browse existing ones.
3. Invite members.
4. Manage requests or membership.
5. Use the squad for tournaments if the tournament supports it.

### Guides

Use `Guides` to:

Open `Achievements` to view:

- your progress
- points
- unlocked items
- certificates or completion-related outputs when available

## 17. Manage Your Profile

Use `Profile` to:

- update your account details
- manage your public profile
- review social activity
- manage security settings
- interact with follows or profile-based actions

### If role switching is available

Some users can switch between `Player` and another approved role.
If that happens, the dashboard and available tools may change.

## 18. Common Player Problems

### I can browse booking but cannot finish the booking

You probably still need to log in before the booking record can be created.

### I cannot open some pages

Possible reasons:

- you are not logged in
- 2FA is still pending
- the feature is disabled for your account

### I do not see my expected payment result yet

The venue may still be reviewing your payment proof.

### I signed in with social login and the account feels incomplete

You may still need:

- username setup
- email completion
- 2FA completion depending on the sign-in path

## 19. Recommended First-Day Checklist

1. Create your account.
2. Verify your email if you used email signup.
3. Log in.
4. Complete 2FA if asked.
5. Finish your profile and username.
6. Explore `Booking`.
7. Make your first booking.
8. Check `My Bookings` and your receipt.
9. Explore `Community`, `Groups`, `Partners`, and `Messages`.
10. Check `Tournaments`, `Squads`, `Coaches`, `Academy`, and `Guides`.
