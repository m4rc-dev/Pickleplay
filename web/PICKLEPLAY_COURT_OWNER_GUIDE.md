# PicklePlay Court Owner Guide

This guide is for PicklePlay users who manage venues, courts, pricing, schedules, bookings, tournaments, and related operations as a court owner.

## Who This Guide Is For

Use this guide if your account is a `Court Owner` and you need to manage:

- locations and courts
- booking operations
- court schedules and events
- pricing and transactions
- revenue-related views
- tournaments
- court managers
- owner-specific profile and operational tasks

## Important Notes Before You Start

1. Your visible menu depends on your active role and enabled features.
2. Some owner-only pages may be hidden if the feature is disabled.
3. If you have multiple approved roles, make sure you switch into `Court Owner` mode before starting owner work.
4. If 2FA is pending, many pages will redirect you to the verification screen first.

## 1. Sign In and Confirm You Are in Court Owner Mode

1. Open PicklePlay and log in.
2. Complete email verification if needed.
3. Complete 2FA if the app asks for it.
4. Open your profile or role switcher.
5. Confirm your active role is `Court Owner`.

If you stay in `Player` mode, you may not see the court owner menu.

## 2. Main Court Owner Navigation

Common court owner areas include:

- `Dashboard`
- `My Courts`
- `Court Bookings`
- `Court Events`
- `Manage Tournaments`
- `Revenue Analytics`
- `Transactions`
- `Court Pricing`
- `Profile`

Some setups may also include access to `Court Policies`.

## 3. Use the Court Owner Dashboard

The owner dashboard is usually the best starting point for daily review.

### What you may see

- operational status
- verification or application-related reminders
- shortcuts into courts, bookings, and tournaments
- manager-related items
- owner-specific business activity

### Recommended dashboard routine

1. Open `Dashboard`.
2. Review anything pending.
3. Jump into the action area that needs attention first.

## 4. Manage Locations and Courts

Open `My Courts` to manage venue records.

### Common tasks

- view all owned locations
- open a location detail page
- add or edit court information
- update court settings
- review the current operating setup for each court

### Basic workflow

1. Open `My Courts`.
2. Review your locations.
3. Open a location.
4. Add or edit the necessary court information.
5. Save your changes.

### Practical advice

- Keep court names consistent and easy to understand.
- Review availability and pricing after editing a court.
- If a page is missing from the owner menu, check whether the feature is enabled for your role.

## 5. Manage Court Bookings

Open `Court Bookings` to monitor booking activity from the owner side.

### Common owner tasks

- review incoming bookings
- check booking states
- review payment-related details
- handle operational follow-up for reservations

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Court Bookings`.
2. Review new or pending bookings first.
3. Check booking details and payment information.
4. Process the booking according to your venue workflow.

## 6. Manage Court Events and Schedules

Open `Court Events` to manage schedule-related activity.

### You may use this area to

- review the calendar
- create schedule blocks
- manage closures or event timing
- keep the court schedule aligned with real availability

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Court Events`.
2. Review the current calendar.
3. Add or edit schedule items.
4. Save the changes.
5. Recheck booking impact if needed.

## 7. Manage Court Pricing

Use `Court Pricing` to control how court time is priced.

### Common pricing tasks

- review existing price rules
- update hourly pricing
- adjust schedule-based pricing if supported
- save new pricing logic

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Court Pricing`.
2. Review current price settings.
3. Update the relevant values.
4. Save your changes.
5. Recheck booking behavior if pricing changes are significant.

## 8. Review Transactions

Use `Transactions` to monitor money-related activity tied to your venue operations.

### You may use this section to

- review transaction records
- inspect payment-related entries
- track items that still need follow-up

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Transactions`.
2. Review anything pending first.
3. Cross-check with related bookings when needed.

## 9. Review Revenue Analytics

Open `Revenue Analytics` if it is enabled for your role.

### What this section is for

- monitoring venue performance
- reviewing booking-related revenue activity
- understanding court business patterns over time

### Recommended use

1. Review your revenue dashboard regularly.
2. Compare results with bookings and pricing changes.
3. Use the trends to decide whether schedule or pricing adjustments are needed.

## 10. Manage Tournaments

Open `Manage Tournaments` for tournament operations.

### What you may do there

- view tournament listings
- create tournaments
- open tournament management pages
- manage deeper tournament workflows

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Manage Tournaments`.
2. Review your tournament list.
3. Create a tournament or open an existing one.
4. Manage the event from the detailed page or management hub.

### Important note

Some tournaments may use individual registrations while others depend on squad registrations.

## 11. Manage Court Managers

Court owners may also handle court manager assignment and approval.

### Typical manager-related tasks

- invite a manager
- review manager status
- approve or assign a manager
- remove a manager if needed

### Basic workflow

1. Open the relevant court or management page.
2. Review current manager information.
3. Start an invite or assignment flow.
4. Approve or update the manager relationship as needed.

## 12. Use Court Policies

Some owner setups include a `Court Policies` page.

### This area may be used to

- define venue rules
- review policy text
- manage policy-related operational guidance

This page may appear through the locations flow instead of as a main sidebar item.

## 13. Use Your Owner Profile

Open `Profile` to manage your account.

### Common owner profile tasks

- review your role status
- manage security settings
- update account details
- switch roles when your account supports it

### If you need to book as a normal player

You may need to switch back to `Player` mode first before using the player-side booking flow normally.

## 14. Common Court Owner Problems

### I do not see my owner tools

Check the following:

1. Your account is approved as a court owner.
2. Your active role is `Court Owner`.
3. The feature is enabled for your role.
4. 2FA is not still pending.

### I can log in but I still do not see a specific page

The page may be:

- disabled by feature access
- hidden until your role is fully approved
- available only through another owner page

### I only see player pages

Switch back into `Court Owner` mode if role switching is available.

## 15. Recommended Daily Owner Workflow

1. Open `Dashboard`.
2. Review new operational items.
3. Check `Court Bookings`.
4. Review `Court Events` and schedule changes.
5. Check `Transactions`.
6. Update `Court Pricing` if needed.
7. Review tournament or manager-related tasks.
8. Check `Revenue Analytics` for business tracking.
