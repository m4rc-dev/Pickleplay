# PicklePlay Coach Guide

This guide is for PicklePlay users who coach players, run clinics, and manage coaching schedules.

## Who This Guide Is For

Use this guide if your account is a `Coach` and you need to manage:

- students
- clinics
- lesson schedules
- coaching-related profile activity

## Important Notes Before You Start

1. Your visible menu depends on your active role and enabled features.
2. If your account has multiple roles, confirm you are in `Coach` mode before starting coach tasks.
3. If 2FA is pending, many pages will redirect you to the verification screen first.

## 1. Log In and Confirm Your Role

1. Open PicklePlay and log in.
2. Complete email verification if needed.
3. Complete 2FA if prompted.
4. Confirm that your active role is `Coach`.

If you remain in `Player` mode, your coach tools may not appear.

## 2. Main Coach Navigation

Common coach sections include:

- `Dashboard`
- `My Students`
- `Manage Clinics`
- `Lesson Schedule`
- `Profile`

You may also still see general user areas like community or tournaments depending on your account permissions.

## 3. Use the Coach Dashboard

The coach dashboard is the best place to start your day.

### It may highlight

- student counts
- clinic activity
- lesson requests
- schedule-related shortcuts

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Dashboard`.
2. Review current coaching activity.
3. Move into students, clinics, or schedule depending on priority.

## 4. Manage My Students

Open `My Students` to review the learners connected to your coaching work.

### Common uses

- review your student list
- see students connected through lessons or clinics
- monitor coaching-related participation

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `My Students`.
2. Review the latest student activity.
3. Identify who needs follow-up or scheduling.

## 5. Manage Clinics

Open `Manage Clinics` for clinic operations.

### Common clinic tasks

- create clinics
- review clinic listings
- manage participants
- update clinic details

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Manage Clinics`.
2. Review active clinics.
3. Create a new clinic or update an existing one.
4. Check participant information.
5. Save changes.

## 6. Manage Lesson Schedule

Open `Lesson Schedule` for coaching calendar work.

### You may use this area to

- review your lesson calendar
- check lesson requests
- manage time-based coaching activity

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Lesson Schedule`.
2. Review upcoming lessons and requests.
3. Update your schedule as needed.
4. Recheck for conflicts or follow-up items.

## 7. Use Your Coach Profile

Open `Profile` to manage your account.

### Common coach profile tasks

- update profile information
- manage security settings
- review your public-facing account details
- switch roles if your account supports more than one role

## 8. Use Player Features When Available

Some coach accounts may also use standard player features such as:

- community
- tournaments
- squads
- profile-based social interaction

Whether these appear depends on your enabled features and role setup.

## 9. Common Coach Problems

### I do not see coach pages

Check the following:

1. Your account is approved as a coach.
2. Your active role is `Coach`.
3. The coach features are enabled for your role.
4. 2FA is not still pending.

### I only see player features

Switch back into `Coach` mode if role switching is available.

### I opened a coach page and it looks empty

That may mean:

- you do not have linked student, clinic, or lesson data yet
- your coach setup is still incomplete
- the page is available but your data has not been populated yet

## 10. Recommended Daily Coach Workflow

1. Open `Dashboard`.
2. Review `My Students`.
3. Check `Manage Clinics`.
4. Review `Lesson Schedule`.
5. Use `Profile` for updates or security changes.
