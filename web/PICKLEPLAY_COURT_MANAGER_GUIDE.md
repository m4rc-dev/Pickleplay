# PicklePlay Court Manager Guide

This guide is for PicklePlay users who operate one assigned court or venue area as a `Court Manager`.

## Who This Guide Is For

Use this guide if your work is focused on:

- reviewing the assigned court
- managing day-to-day booking operations
- updating the court schedule
- supporting venue operations for a specific court

## Important Notes Before You Start

1. Court managers usually have a narrower scope than court owners.
2. You may only manage the assigned court, not the full venue business.
3. Revenue tools, full owner settings, and broader pricing controls may not be available to you.
4. If 2FA is pending, many pages will redirect you to the verification screen first.

## 1. Complete the Manager Onboarding Flow

Court managers often enter PicklePlay through an invitation.

### Typical onboarding flow

1. Open the manager invite link.
2. Review the invitation details.
3. Register or log in using the invited email address.
4. Accept the manager-related flow.
5. Wait for owner approval if that is part of your setup.

If login is blocked, your invitation may still be incomplete or waiting for approval.

## 2. Log In and Confirm You Are in Court Manager Mode

1. Open PicklePlay and log in.
2. Complete email verification if needed.
3. Complete 2FA if prompted.
4. Confirm that your active role is `Court Manager`.

Managers are often redirected straight to the court manager area instead of the normal player dashboard.

## 3. Main Court Manager Navigation

Common court manager sections include:

- `Overview`
- `Assigned Court`
- `Court Bookings`
- `Court Schedule`

You may also still see shared sections like `News` or `Profile` depending on your configuration.

## 4. Use the Overview Page

The `Overview` page is your daily starting point.

### It may help you

- review assigned-court context
- check current operations
- jump into bookings or schedule tasks

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Overview`.
2. Review current court activity.
3. Move next into bookings or schedule work.

## 5. Review the Assigned Court

Use `Assigned Court` to focus on the specific court you manage.

### Common uses

- confirm which court is assigned to you
- review the current court setup
- understand the operational context for your daily tasks

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Assigned Court`.
2. Review the court details.
3. Confirm you are working on the correct court before changing anything else.

## 6. Manage Court Bookings

Open `Court Bookings` to handle the daily booking workload.

### Common manager tasks

- review booking records
- inspect booking details
- support booking-related operational actions
- monitor current activity for the assigned court

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Court Bookings`.
2. Review new or active bookings first.
3. Check booking details carefully.
4. Perform the operational actions available to your role.

## 7. Manage the Court Schedule

Open `Court Schedule` to manage availability-related operations.

### This area may be used to

- review the schedule
- add or update operational time blocks
- manage closures or changes
- keep real availability aligned with the booking experience

### Suggested workflow

1. Open `Court Schedule`.
2. Review the calendar or time blocks.
3. Update the schedule as needed.
4. Save the changes.
5. Recheck nearby booking impact when necessary.

## 8. Use Your Profile

Even as a manager, you may still need to manage your personal account.

### Common profile tasks

- update account details
- manage security settings
- review role-related information

If you have multiple roles, confirm you are in the correct role before doing manager work.

## 9. What Court Managers Usually Cannot Do

Compared with court owners, managers may not have access to:

- full venue-wide business settings
- revenue analytics
- full transaction oversight
- owner-level pricing tools
- broader tournament administration
- court ownership or policy controls

This depends on the actual feature setup, but managers are typically operations-focused.

## 10. Common Court Manager Problems

### I cannot access the manager area

Possible reasons:

1. The invite flow is not complete yet.
2. The owner has not approved your manager access yet.
3. You logged in with a different email from the invited one.
4. 2FA is still pending.

### I only see player pages

If role switching is available, switch into `Court Manager` mode first.

### I do not see business-wide controls

That is usually expected. Court managers normally have limited operational access only.

## 11. Recommended Daily Manager Workflow

1. Open `Overview`.
2. Review your assigned court.
3. Check `Court Bookings`.
4. Update `Court Schedule` if needed.
5. Return to bookings for any follow-up.
6. Use `Profile` for any account or security updates.
