# PicklePlay Role Guides

This file is a quick index for the separate PicklePlay role-based documents.

## Available Guides

- `PICKLEPLAY_PLAYER_GUIDE.md`
  - for players who book courts, use the community, find partners, join tournaments, and manage their profile

- `PICKLEPLAY_COURT_OWNER_GUIDE.md`
  - for court owners who manage locations, bookings, schedules, pricing, tournaments, managers, and owner operations

- `PICKLEPLAY_COURT_MANAGER_GUIDE.md`
  - for court managers who handle assigned-court operations, bookings, and schedule work

- `PICKLEPLAY_COACH_GUIDE.md`
  - for coaches who manage students, clinics, schedules, and coaching-related activity

- `PICKLEPLAY_ADMIN_GUIDE.md`
  - for admins who manage platform-wide tools such as verifications, applications, security, maintenance, and admin operations

## Suggested Usage

- Send the `Player` guide to regular end users.
- Send the `Court Owner` guide to venue owners or operational heads.
- Send the `Court Manager` guide to staff assigned to daily court operations.
- Send the `Coach` guide to instructors and clinic managers.
- Send the `Admin` guide only to internal admin users.
